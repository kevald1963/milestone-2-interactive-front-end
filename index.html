Introduction

Use this app to help you to:

  • Find and display routes between various locations in Tyne & Wear and the hospitals that serve the county.

  • Mark points on the map to show locations of bus stations, metro stations, hospitals, etc.

Get started!

First select the area from which you want to depart from the Departure Area menu above. You will then be able to find routes and show markers on the map.

On here you can plot routes from various departure points in your borough to any of the hospitals in and around Tyne & Wear. Routes can be shown for journeys by public transport, cycling, walking or driving.

Please select a departure point, the hospital you want to go, and a travel mode, then click Find Route to display the route.

Note that only one route is shown at a time by default. To leave each new route displayed every time you click 'Find Route', then first check the 'Allow multiple routes' checkbox.
