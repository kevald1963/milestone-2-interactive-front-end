Welcome!

This app finds and displays routes on a Google Map between the major districts of Tyne & Wear and the hospitals that serve the county. Routes can be shown for journeys by public transport, cycling, walking or driving.

To show a route on the map, first select the area from which you want to depart from the menu above.

Please select a departure point, the hospital you want to go, and a travel mode, then click Find Route to display the route.
